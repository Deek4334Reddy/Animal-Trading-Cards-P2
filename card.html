Emperor Penguin

Emperor Penguins are the tallest species of penguins, standing nearly 4 feet tall.

  • Scientific Name: Aptenodytes forsteri
  • Average Height: 1.1 – 1.3 m (Adult)
  • Average Weight: 27 - 41 kg
  • Average Life Span: 20 - 25 years

But these species are dwarfed by the ancient ‘mega-penguins’. Fossils recovered from the Antarctic Peninsula reveal that a colossus species of penguin which lived about 37 million years ago may have stood 2m tall and weighed as much as 115 kg.
